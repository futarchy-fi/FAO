How It Works

FAO uses prediction markets to make governance decisions. Token holders propose changes, markets price them, and the best outcomes win.

1

Token & Sale

Buy FAO via a fixed-price initial phase or a bonding curve. Proceeds flow to the protocol treasury and liquidity pools on Gnosis Chain.

2

Proposals

Anyone can create governance proposals through the FutarchyOfficialProposalSource. Candidates are promoted to official status by the authorized proposer.

3

Markets

Each proposal spawns YES/NO conditional tokens that trade on Swapr. The market price represents the crowd's probability estimate of each outcome.

4

Settlement

When markets resolve, the winning outcome executes or rejects the proposal. 80% of liquidity migrates to conditional pools during active proposals.

5

Ragequit

Burn FAO at any time to exit with your pro-rata share of the treasury. Eligible assets include xDAI and any whitelisted ragequit tokens.

Sale Mechanics

FAO tokens are minted via a two-phase sale on Gnosis Chain.

1.0x Buyer
0.5x Treasury
0.2x Incentive
0.3x Insider
Buyer (1.0x) Treasury (0.5x) Incentive (0.2x) Insider (0.3x)

Bonding Curve

After the fixed-price initial phase, the sale transitions to a bonding curve. The price increases as more tokens are sold, rewarding early participants while ensuring continuous availability.

  • Initial phase: fixed price per token
  • Curve phase: price rises with supply
  • Sale funds split across buyer allocation, treasury, incentives, and insider vesting

Ragequit

FAO holders can burn their tokens at any time to receive a pro-rata share of the treasury's eligible assets. This exit mechanism ensures the token always has fundamental backing.

  • Burn FAO to receive xDAI and eligible tokens
  • Pro-rata share based on total supply at time of exit
  • Eligible tokens managed via ragequitTokensLength()

Governance & Arbitration

Futarchy-based governance powered by conditional token markets on Swapr.

Proposal Flow

Proposals move through the FutarchyOfficialProposalSource lifecycle:

  • Candidate — Anyone submits a proposal
  • Promote — Authorized proposer promotes to official
  • Markets — YES/NO conditional tokens created and traded
  • Migrate — 80% of spot liquidity moves to conditional pools
  • Settle — CTF oracle resolves the outcome

Liquidity Migration

When a proposal becomes active, the FutarchyLiquidityManager migrates 80% of spot liquidity into conditional YES/NO pools on Swapr.

  • Spot pool retains 20% as baseline
  • Conditional pools split across YES_FAO/YES_WXDAI and NO_FAO/NO_WXDAI
  • After settlement, liquidity migrates back to spot
  • Emergency exit available with timelock safeguard
