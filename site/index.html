What FAO is, how the sale works, and where to learn more.

Sale mechanics

  • Two-week fixed-price phase at 0.0001 ETH per FAO.
  • Post-finalization: linear bonding curve based on net sales.
  • Mint splits: 1.0× buyer, 0.5× treasury, 0.2× incentive, 0.3× insider vesting.

Ragequit rules

  • Burn FAO to redeem ETH and ERC20s held by the sale contract.
  • Initial-phase redemptions reduce the recorded initial sale tally.
  • Admin actions use governance controls and role-based authorization.
